Predicting steel-plates defects

I joined a Kaggle playground series competition with the task of predicting steel-plate defects. Submissions were evaluated using area under the ROC curve for each of the defect categories and then averaged to give a final score.

I achieved a area under ROC score of 0.86375, which ranked me in the 65th percentile (1443 out of 2199). The top score of the competition achieved a 0.89782 score.

Tech Stack:

Python
Sci-kit Learn
Jupyter Notebook
Pyspark

Project tag: Big data

Prediction, Image processing & Transfer learning
