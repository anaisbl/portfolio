Computer science

My journey into technology, specifically 'coding' began in 2019, with an elective class during my undergrad called Business Information Systems- glorified name for what was essentially, an introduction to coding fundamentals. This was eye-opening, it made me realise that coding wasn't as hard as I always believed it to be. My interest was piqued.

After graduating, I looked up a few tutorials online to learn to code, and started down the path of web development. A year later, I wanted more: I wanted to learn how computers work, how data is stored, how tools are built. This led me to pursue and obtain a Master of Science in Computer Science with a specialisation in Data science.

As of 2025, I am a fresh graduate from the Technological University of Dublin currently working as a Workflow AI Engineer at BoundlessHQ. Knowledge and skills from outside the tech world have been invaluable to my journey so far, and I am excited to continue learning and growing in dynamic industry!
